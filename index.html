ABOUT

The NYU Abu Dhabi Art Center is a vibrant hub for the performing arts, located at the heart of NYUAD's Saadiyat Island campus. It serves as a platform for international and local artists to showcase their work, while providing students and the broader community with access to world-class performances and workshops. Since its inception, the center has hosted numerous artists and productions, ranging from theater, music, and dance to multimedia art installations.

By bridging the local and global art communities, the NYUAD Art Center plays a critical role in the cultural landscape of Abu Dhabi, promoting cross-cultural dialogue and artistic expression in a diverse and ever-evolving world.
