ABOUT

The NYU Abu Dhabi Art Center is an exciting place for performing arts, located at the center of NYUAD's Saadiyat Island campus. It offers a space for both international and local artists to present their work, giving students and the community the chance to experience high-quality performances and take part in workshops.

The NYUAD Art Center plays an important role in Abu Dhabi’s cultural scene by connecting local and global art communities. It encourages conversations across different cultures and supports artistic expression in a diverse and changing world.
